Fry Bread

Ingredients

  • 8 cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 Tbsp. of Baking Powder
  • 4 1/2 tsp. of salt
  • 1/8 cup of Honey
  • 6-8 cups of Warm Water
  • 1 Liter of Canola Oil (Or Shortening)

Directions

Mix dry ingredients (Flour, Baking Power and Salt) by hand in a large bowl.

Drizzle the Honey into the bottom of a glass 8-cup measuring cup. Add very warm water into the cup and mix the Honey until disolved.

Note: When humidity is high, use less water.

Do not overhandle when kneeding the bread dough. The consistancy needs to remain soft and slightly sticky.

Cover and let dough rest for 1.5-2 hours.

Frying the Dough

  1. Pre-heat stove-top to 350F (175C) and add your shortening or oil to a deep pan or pot.
  2. With floured hands, shape about 1/2 cup of dough into 1-inch patties. Create a hole in the middle for consistant frying.
  3. Gently lower one piece at a time into the hot oil, waiting 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Place the fresh Frybread onto a surface that can drain or absorb excess oil.

Prepare your Fry Bread

For a Sweet Desert: top with honey and powdered sugar.

For Indian Tacos: Top with Chile and your choice of taco toppings.
