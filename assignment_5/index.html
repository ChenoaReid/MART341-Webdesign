DICEBERG Ahead!

Making TTRPG Podacasts

DICEBERG Ahead! is a actual play podcast where our cast plays tabletop roleplay games of all different systems.

Starting in 2019 with the podcast campaign REDROCK, our cast played on a fantasy mars using the Edge Studio's Genesys System. Later, we added the campaign Emergence, using Dungeons and Dragon 5th edition.
